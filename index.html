Quality Control

Every single Falcon Steel tube undergoes a rigorous, multi-stage testing protocol. We utilize advanced metallurgical and mechanical testing facilities to guarantee international compliance, superior strength, and ultimate durability for the most demanding applications.

Hydrostatic Test
